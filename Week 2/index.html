Places I've Lived

I am fortunate to have been able to call a variety of places around the world home, even if for a brief period.
Here are 6 of the places I spent at least two months in!

  1. I grew up in Plainsboro, NJ. As the name suggests, the town was plain and the land was open and expansive. I have many fond memories of running around the backyard with my sibilings and riding bikes with my neighborhood friends.
  2. Zooming out, I moved to Hong Kong after fourth grade, and spent the next five years in this exciting city.
  3. Next, I finished up my last three years of high school in Massachusetts.
  4. After high school, I was excited to attend UCLA and explore the coast I had never been to! I spent my first year at UCLA in Dykstra Hall.
  5. After my second year at UCLA, I went abroad for the summer to study physics at the University of Nicosia in the island country of Cyprus.
  6. Although it has been bittersweet to be finishing my last year of college from home, it has been nice to spend some more time with family back in Princeton, NJ.
