US-China Plastic Waste Trade, 2016 2017

Differential impacts of global plastic waste

Prior to China's ban on importing foreign plastic waste in 2017, much of the United States' plastic waste entered China via the ports of Hong Kong.

Circles are proportional to the magnitude of plastic being imported and exported from each country.

Red represents the import of plastic waste, while green represents plastic leaving the country through exports.
